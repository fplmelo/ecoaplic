Response component

Screening response

    • Normal
    • Poisson

The explanatory (linear component)

  • Can be additive when independent
    • y = a + b1x1 + b2x2…
  • Can be multiplicative
    • y = a + b1x1 * b2x2

Linear Models (mean)

  • Statistish: y = Xβ + ε
  • R syntax: lm(y~1)
